Welcome to Social Activity Club

Aim:

जिव्हाळा is the art of connecting yourself to the souls in need, to feel their sorrows, to sense their emptiness. The club urges to help the people in need and make the society a better place to live in.

Objectives

To generate awareness about the contemporary social and environmental issues in the context of sustainability and social responsibility.

It focuses on sustainable development and charity work with the passion for volunteerism and inciting change.
