Welcome to Techno-Vishkar Club

Aim:

To create awareness, educate, nurture and inculcate a culture of innovation amongst the engineering student.

Objectives

To provide various advancements of knowledge in basic and applied areas of engineering and technology.

To enable them to generate new ideas and become more innovative.

To Conduct Various technical workshops for the students by the students and by technical experts in respective fields.

To provide platform to students for various opportunities to show and enhance our technical talents at state, national and international levels.
